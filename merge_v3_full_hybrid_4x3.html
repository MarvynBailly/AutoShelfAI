Ripple effects & risks

The four risks we sleep with.

AutoShelfAI · Agentic-AI Challenge

! False positives waste associate time

A noisy system drowns a manager in notifications and erodes trust — the failure mode that killed Bossa Nova's value at Walmart.

Mitigation: every refill task needs a 1-tap human accept. Severity tiers + per-shift cap + revenue-weighted ranking keep alerts focused.

! Customer privacy — faces in shelf photos

Shelf captures sometimes include customers in the background. Policy and perception both matter.

Mitigation: faces blurred on-device before any image leaves the phone. Photos encrypted and deleted after audit. GDPR and CCPA compliant by default.

! "It's going to take my job"

The obvious objection. Our answer isn't "it won't" — it's "here's what your job becomes."

Mitigation: associates shift from audit drudgery to customer-facing work. Pilot will validate associate preference directly.

! Still works when the cloud AI is down

Enterprise reliability can't depend on a third-party service's uptime. If the cloud AI is unreachable, the audit must still happen.

Mitigation: a local-only audit still runs using the lightweight on-device tools — no cloud call, same loop. Built-in image cleanup for bad photos. Reliability is a first-class path, not an afterthought.

Upstream — what we ingest

↑Planogram

Tells us which product belongs in which shelf position, so we move from guessing brands to knowing exact items.

↑POS / sales data

Revenue-weight every gap so the refill checklist is ranked by dollars, not alphabetical order.

↑Warehouse inventory

Distinguish a shelf-replenishment problem from a stockroom-empty problem.

Downstream — what we feed

↓Associate task app

Every gap becomes a task in the app associates already use — with product, location, and quantity pre-filled.

↓Auto-reorder

Chronic out-of-stock items auto-trigger replenishment in the system the store already runs.

↓Manager dashboard

Per-store shelf availability and loss-prevention analytics roll into tools managers already check.
